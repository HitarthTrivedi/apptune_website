Features

Built for macOS.
Feels native.

App-triggered playback
AppTune watches every app launch and focus event system-wide using NSWorkspace. The moment you open an app that has a rule, your music starts — instantly, silently, automatically.
→App launched: Xcode
Matching rule found...
→Playing "Deep Focus" on Spotify
→App launched: Notion
Matching rule found...
→Playing "Lo-fi Beats" on Apple Music
Spotify OAuth
Sign in with your real Spotify account. AppTune fetches all your personal playlists via the Web API and stores your token securely in the macOS Keychain.
Apple Music ready
Your full Apple Music library is available instantly. No API keys, no setup — AppTune uses the native Music app via AppleScript bridge.
Lives in your menu bar
AppTune has zero Dock presence. It runs silently as a menu bar app, launching at login via SMAppService — the modern macOS Login Items API.
Two trigger modes
Trigger music on app launch, on app focus, or both. Each rule is independently configurable so you have complete control over when music plays.
